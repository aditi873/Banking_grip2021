About Us

We provide great services and ideas

Imperial Bank, a Fortune 500 company, is an Indian Multinational, Public Sector Banking and Financial services statutory body headquartered in Mumbai. The rich heritage and legacy of over 200 years, accredits Imperial Bank as the most trusted Bank by Indians through generations. Imperial Bank, the largest Indian Bank with 1/4th market share, serves over 44 crore customers through its vast network of over 22,000 branches, 58,500 ATMs, 66,000 BC outlets, with an undeterred focus on innovation, and customer centricity, which stems from the core values of the Bank - Service, Transparency, Ethics, Politeness and Sustainability. The Bank has successfully diversified businesses through its 11 subsidiaries i.e Imperial Bank General Insurance, Imperial Bank Life Insurance, Imperial Bank Mutual Fund, Imperial Bank Card, etc. It has spread its presence globally and operates across time zones through 233 offices in 32 foreign countries. Growing with times, Imperial Bank continues to redefine banking in India, as it aims to offer responsible and sustainable Banking solutions.
